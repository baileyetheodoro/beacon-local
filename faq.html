Project policies

What to know.

These answers are a starting point. Your proposal and agreement will confirm the exact scope for your project.

Current draft rate: copywriting and repair work outside the agreed project scope is billed at $75 per hour.
Do I need to provide the website content?

Yes. Clients provide the core information, service details, business facts, photographs, and other materials needed for the website. Beacon Local will organize the supplied content so it works clearly within the design.

Copywriting or substantial rewriting is available as an additional service billed at $75 per hour.

Can I update the website myself after launch?

This depends on the platform and the type of website delivered. When self-editing is available, Beacon Local can explain which areas are appropriate for routine updates.

Changes made by the client or another provider after handoff are not covered as corrections to the original project. If those changes damage the layout or functionality, repair work is billed at $75 per hour and depends on an available backup or the extent of the issue.

Who owns the website, domain, and hosting accounts?

The client should own and pay for the domain and hosting accounts directly. Beacon Local can assist with setup, connection, transfer, and launch while keeping those accounts in the client’s name.

What is included in a revision round?

One revision round is one consolidated list of requested changes submitted after reviewing the website preview. Requests should relate to the approved project scope.

Additional revision rounds, new pages, or changes in direction may require a separate quote.

What happens if I need more pages or features?

Additional pages and functionality can be quoted separately. Beacon Local will explain any change in price or timeline for approval before completing work outside the original scope.

What support is available after launch?

Website Care is available for $99 per month and includes up to 30 minutes of content updates, minor edits, basic maintenance, and email support each month. Work beyond the plan allowance is billed separately.
